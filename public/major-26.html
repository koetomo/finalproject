Statistics

Description:

Statistics is the study of the collection, organization, analysis, interpretation and presentation of data.It deals with all aspects of data, including the planning of data collection in terms of the design of surveys and experiments. The word statistics, when referring to the scientific discipline, is singular, as in "Statistics is an art."This should not be confused with the word statistic, referring to a quantity (such as mean or median) calculated from a set of data,whose plural is statistics ("this statistic seems wrong" or "these statistics are misleading").
