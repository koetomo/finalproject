Description:

Spanish , also called Castilian (castellano About this sound listen ), is a Romance language that originated in Castile, a region in Spain. Approximately 406 million people speak Spanish as a native language, making it second only to Mandarin in terms of its number of native speakers.It also has 60 million speakers as a second language, and 20 million students as a foreign language. Spanish is one of the six official languages of the United Nations, and is used as an official language by the European Union and Mercosur.

Portuguese is a Romance language. It is the official language of Portugal, Brazil, Mozambique, Angola, Cape Verde, Guinea-Bissau, and São Tomé and Príncipe.Portuguese has co-official status in Macau in East Asia and East Timor in Southeast Asia. As the result of expansion during colonial times, Portuguese speakers are also found in Goa, Daman and Diu in India,and in Malacca in Malaysia.
